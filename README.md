## Project Information

This Physician's Office System will be a software designed to support daily operations of a doctor’s clinic. It will keep accurate, current records of doctors' schedules, patient data, staff information, and clinical visits. This will help office staff and physicians manage their work more efficiently and lead to a more organized approach to scheduling and data management.

This app is primarily designed for physicians, patients, and office staff, and it would allow the office to go paperless in its approach to storing data. Physicians will use the system to review patient histories, record clinical notes, diagnoses, and prescriptions, and manage their appointment schedules, for example, by viewing their fixed timetables, adding tasks or limited leave on available time slots, and submitting rescheduling requests to office staff when necessary. Office staff will use the system to register patients, schedule and manage appointments, and maintain and update patient information. Office staff will also be able to accept a patient's request for an appointment with a particular physician. Patients will also be able to access limited functionality, such as booking/requesting an appointment with a particular physician, viewing appointment details, and visit summaries. Patients can also input more health-related information about themselves that they would like the doctor to go through before the appointment such as allergy information. 

The system will maintain electronic patient records that include personal information, appointment history, and visit documentation. It will support appointment scheduling while preventing conflicts in physician availability. Access to information will be controlled through role-based security, ensuring that sensitive medical data is only accessible to authorized users. This role-based access will be given at the start via login and authentication. Only patients will be allowed to create an account freely; however, due to security, only the admin can add the doctor's and office staff's information to the database, and they will have more functionality than patients in this app. This system, however, will not provide basic or advanced diagnostic or treatment decision support. Its focus is on improving workflow efficiency within a physician’s office rather than supporting complex hospital environments.
